About Us

Ever since Industrialization started the care for plants has made it to our priority list, Even though mankind requires plants to balance the food chain that he's a part of, but the plant enthusiasts are everywhere,be it big metropolitan cities or rural areas,so we aim to bring in platform that can connect these plants/nature enthusiast and help them exchange or sell plants or donate seeds,plants as part of the green initiative that we started two years back. The platform offers its customers all types of young plants that are local,imported and exotic.It is user friendly where customers can place their order from home just by creating user account and they can choose the plant,quantity and confirm their order just in a click.
